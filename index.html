about me

I am currently in my 2nd year, heading for my 3rd year next winter - yeah, it's kinda funky -. I'm looking for COOP opportunities this next summer in the webdev and robotics/IoT industries.

As for now, I am VP Social & Communications at uOttawa SESA (Software Engineering Student Association). I've been part of this organization from my first year, during which I worked mainly on translating communications from English to French and communicating events organized by the organization to our fellow engineering students.

Outside of school and programming, I like to read about history, political sciences and technology, whether it is in physical books, newspapers, or HackerNews and Medium. Also, I like to play hockey - I'm a defenseman -, watch soccer (German fan here) and to play the drums. My favourite bands would be Rush (you know... Tom Sawyer? Or if you dare, 2112... yeah, it's 20mins long, but you gotta do what you gotta do!), Nirvana (and pretty much all other grunge/alt rock of the 90s) and Jean Leloup (yay, good stuff from Québec).

I also watch some shows, like “The Walking Dead” and its spinoff “Fear the Walking Dead”, “Silicon Valley” and “Mr. Robot”, and game on my PC from time to time with friends.
